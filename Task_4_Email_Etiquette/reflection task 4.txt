Task 4 Write-up: Communication and Etiquette 
This task was mainly focussed of the importance of professional digital communication. I drafted two formal emails where one was about requesting the professor to extend the deadline for submission of assignment and the other was about sending an email to an internship coordinator to express my interest to join the summer internship. The main important thing to keep in mind while writing these emails was to maintain a respectful and professional tone. 
Later I also developed a social media do’s and dont’s list to stay safe in the online world.
One of the scenarios which taught me importance of communication was when me and my friend could have lost friendship due to the communication gap. We were really good friends but the thing was we used to chat more than meeting in person nor did we call. Slowly we stopped chatting and thus when I asked her to meet in person, she thought I meant something else as my reason was not clear. My intention was to just have a casual friends meetup. So later I called her and explained what exactly I meant. Before that she even had blocked me as she thought I might h=mean something else. I got unblocked after meeting her on my daily walk one day and then explained that whatever she thought was not true and it was just a casual meetup. She understood what mistake she had made and even I understood that I should have made it clear by making the subject clear for the meetup. We then were almost at the edge of losing our precious friendship but got saved by communicating correctly. From that day on I realized how important communication actually is and we should always be clear with the message.
