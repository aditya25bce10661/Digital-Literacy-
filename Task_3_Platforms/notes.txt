Task 3 Write-up: Coding and Collaboration
The main focus of Task 3 was on coding, technical tools for practice and data collection. For Part A I used HackerRank to compete the “Solve Me First” task and have even completed it successfully. Thus, showing my ability to go through coding programs and solving the problems easily. For Part B, I created a Google Form quiz titled "Digital Literacy & Online Safety" to teach them the basics of the online world and how to remain safe in this dangerous world of internet. The form that I made was a kind of assignment mainly to test their knowledge and also contains scores which would be shown after the form is submitted.
This quiz included multiple-choice questions regarding password security, which I shared with my peers to gather data on their awareness levels. HackerRank will help me prepare for the technical communications and interviews and also improve my knowledge of coding too while the google workspace will help me to form vital team-based projects in my future careers. The challenge that I faced while making the google form was that the questions disappeared automatically when I clicked the publish button and were not visible in the form. I resolved it by changing my network connection from college Wi-Fi to my mobile data and it worked well enough.
