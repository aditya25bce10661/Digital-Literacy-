Task 5 Write-up: Cybercrime Awareness
Researching Cyberbullying for my case study was eye-opening. I was surprised to learn how quickly a small online disagreement can escalate into a severe mental health crisis when shared across wide social networks. It highlighted that the 24/7 nature of digital harassment makes it far more pervasive than traditional bullying.
As a result of this task, I will personally change my habit of leaving my social media profiles public; I have now set my accounts to private and enabled Two-Factor Authentication on all my logins. Understanding that I can report incidents via 1930 or cybercrime.gov.in gives me the confidence to protect myself and my peers.
