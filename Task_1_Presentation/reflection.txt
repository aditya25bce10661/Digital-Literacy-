Task 1 Write-up: Digital literacy infographic
For the First task I created a one-page visual infographic which will make my batch mates understand the core concepts of Digital Literacy. The software that I used for this task is Canva, to design the infographic. The Topics that I mainly covered were what is digital literacy, useful digital tools, safe internet practices, professional online presence. The design follows a modern, "GTA-style" vector aesthetic to make it engaging for college students. 
The thing that I learned while using Canva was to maintain the visual and textual balance when making the infographic so that the design remains clutter free while delivering the message with clarity. The most difficult part while making the infographic was to cut down the huge information to just a few one or two lines while making those lines full of necessary information. Thus, only a crisp and important information can only be displayed as the space is limited and it should be such that the students can read it quickly without consuming much of their time while passing by and looking at the info graph. I enjoyed making the infographic using Canva as the editing process was quite easy and the icons can be dragged by just holding the left click instead of waiting for the move arrows then dragging. Also, the layering feature of Canva made it really fun to create posters and banners.
