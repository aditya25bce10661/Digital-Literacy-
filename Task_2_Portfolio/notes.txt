Task 2 Write-up: Digital Portfolio 
In today’s digital world, the digital presence has become an essential part of all of our lives. This digital presence is now even required for career prospects these days as the recruiters even look at professional profile of a person online. A professional digital presence is very important for tech industry these days. For this task, I established my presence in GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. I chose GitHub for sharing my codes in an online profile where it could be saved and also upload my future coding projects. It can also be used for version control and even store my programming career. LinkedIn is used by mostly all the companies for recruitments. Thus, maintaining my academic journey at VIT Bhopal, and my professional networking, all will be stored in my LinkedIn profile. Kaggle is used to explore data science and machine learning competitions. It is used by programmers mainly to extract a good amount of data sets to train their machine learning programs.
On GitHub, I created a "special" profile README repository to act as my digital business card, listing my branch (CSE Core) and my learning goals. Over the next four years, I plan to use these platforms to host my academic projects, connect with industry experts, and document my growth as a developer. Setting up the account was much simple and thus faced some minor challenges like account info not being updated or some more small issues that I could resolve very quickly.
